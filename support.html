Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions below.

How can I create an IRCTC account login ID?

To create a new IRCTC login ID, visit the official home page of IRCTC. Right below the login box, there is an option to sign up. When you click on it, a registration box will open. Now enter your user ID, password, security question, and preferred language, followed by personal information like name, gender, Aadhaar card number, PAN card number and your residential address. After you enter all the information, click on submit. For the verification of your IRCTC login account, a link will be sent to your registered email ID. Once the registration process is complete you can use your IRCTC account. Watch this video to see all the steps described above.

Can I change my IRCTC login ID?

Creating your IRCTC login ID is a one-time registration process. Once you have registered, you cannot change your username or the login credentials for the registered email address.

How can I log into my IRCTC account?

For IRCTC login, open the ixigo app and just log in using your registered username. Next, select the departure and arrival destinations, date of travel, the class you want to travel in, and your traveller details. Then, click on 'Pay Now' to complete the payment. Once it is successful, the app will ask you to enter your password to complete your booking.

What is the maximum number of IRCTC train tickets one can book at a time?

As per IRCTC, up to 12 bookings are allowed per month and up to 6 seats/berths can be booked per ticket. So, up to 36 tickets can be booked in a month from a single IRCTC User ID.

How do I cancel the IRCTC train ticket after the train has departed?

If a railway booking is confirmed, it can be cancelled up to 48 hours before the scheduled departure of the train. As per the IRCTC website, tickets booked online cannot be cancelled after the chart has been prepared.
